What is popit?

Pop it literally translates as "pop it". This is a child's game, the process of which can be compared to the bursting of an air-bubble wrapping film. But unlike film, pop-its can be pressed with the characteristic sensations and sounds "infinitely": dozens of silicone bubbles are simply squeezed out in the other direction.

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the pimples. It is necessary to finish the series that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 pimples at the same time (they must be nearby, you cannot press several pimples at once if they are not back to back) The task is to make the opponent go last. Whoever goes last loses!
